# Process Improvement Summary

## Current-State Process

Prospect records are entered manually into the CRM-style system. Missing fields are often discovered later when reports are generated or when fundraisers need updated information.

## Pain Points

- Missing giving capacity
- Missing assigned fundraiser
- Missing donor email
- Inconsistent research status
- Manual review required before reporting

## Future-State Process

A data quality script automatically checks prospect records and creates a report of incomplete entries. Staff can review flagged records before reports are shared with fundraising teams.

## Improvement

The automated check reduces manual review and improves confidence in prospect reports.

## Metrics to Track

- Number of incomplete prospect records
- Percent of prospects with assigned fundraisers
- Percent of prospects with giving capacity
- Time saved during report preparation
- Reduction in report rework
